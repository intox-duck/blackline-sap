IR35 & Contractor Psychology
The London market's unique dynamic — Outside IR35 offers 20–30% more take-home pay.
Inside IR35 default. Most large clients blanket-determine "Inside" for risk mitigation, creating friction with experienced contractors who view it as a 25% pay cut versus Outside rates.
Perm "false security." Contractors note that perm roles have zero job security for the first two years and often involve a 25% pay cut compared to Inside contract rates.
BlackLine opportunity. Offering Outside IR35 contracts or high-stability permanent roles with a clear 2-year+ roadmap is essential to attract talent away from blanket Inside determinations at larger financial institutions.
"Twilight of SAP ECC": Demand for SAP Specialists focused on legacy customization has fallen by 21.4%, while SAP Architects have surged +100.6%. London is pivoting from volume ABAP to premium architectural roles.
EPAM & KPMG surge: EPAM Systems saw +162.3% rise in SAP hiring due to its S/4HANA "conversion factory," and KPMG expanded roles by +98.8% through IBM and SAP PartnerEdge partnerships — both actively competing for the same London talent pool.

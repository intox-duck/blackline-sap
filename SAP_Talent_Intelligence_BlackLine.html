GBP-equivalent median comparison
London
Premium market for senior and stakeholder-facing hires
BlackLine-equivalent 8–12 year talent sits at £85k–£120k permanent, with top contractor profiles landing at £600–£750+/day. Use selectively: quality and proximity are strong, but the pool is shrinking and cost is highest.
Kraków
Best overall balance for BlackLine's core engineering build
The 8–12 year target band is now 25–32k PLN/month UoP or 29–36k PLN/month B2B. Tier A SAP quality, CET overlap, and Kraków presence still make this the best composite option.
Mumbai
Depth and cost advantage when BlackLine needs scale
For 8–12 years' experience, BlackLine should benchmark against Product/MNC rates: ₹28–38 LPA, not IT services rates. The trade-off is higher attrition, heavier consulting competition, and weaker timezone overlap for Europe-centric collaboration.

Location takeaway
Prioritise Kraków for core SAP engineering hiring
For BlackLine's current SAP engineering need, Kraków offers the strongest overall mix of delivery quality, CET collaboration, retention profile, and cost. Mumbai should remain the scale market when volume matters. London should be reserved for high-touch leadership or customer-proximate roles where premium cost is justified.
Best composite market

Why Kraków

  • ~30% cheaper than London at the senior median while staying inside the European operating model.
  • Tier A SAP earning potential signals strong technical quality, especially for S/4HANA and Fiori talent.
  • CET timezone alignment fits BlackLine's hybrid Kraków role and makes cross-functional collaboration easier.

Pros

  • Best quality-to-cost balance across the three markets.
  • B2B route gives BlackLine a faster way to secure scarce mid-senior talent.
  • Established Kraków footprint strengthens employer credibility locally.

Cons

  • The addressable pool is much smaller than Mumbai, so sourcing will feel tighter.
  • Demand density is high, which means top candidates will often have multiple options.
  • Comp must land around P50+ and flexibility on B2B is important to stay competitive.

Other considerations

  • Use Mumbai as the secondary market for bench strength, follow-the-sun support, and lower-cost scale.
  • Use London sparingly for principal architects, client-facing SMEs, or roles tied closely to UK/EU stakeholders.
  • Across every location, 90+ day fill times mean speed, career narrative, and technical scope matter as much as cash.
